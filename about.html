Our Foundation

Family. Craftsmanship. Excellence.

G&W Contracting Services LLC was built from the ground up — quite literally. Founded by brothers George and Donnie Mundin, our company is rooted in a shared belief that the work you put your name on should stand the test of time.

Based in Richmond, Virginia, we hold a Class A contractor license from the state — the highest classification available. This means we have the experience, bonding, and qualifications to take on projects of any size and complexity, from residential driveways to full commercial builds.

Our specialty is masonry in all its forms: concrete, brick, block, natural stone, and hardscapes. We take pride in doing the job right the first time, treating every client's property like it's our own, and delivering results that last for generations.

  • Family-owned and operated since day one
  • Class A Licensed, State of Virginia
  • Fully licensed, bonded & insured
  • 25+ years of masonry experience
  • Richmond & Central Virginia
  • Atlanta, GA Metro & Surrounding Counties
  • Residential (70%) and Commercial (30%) expertise
