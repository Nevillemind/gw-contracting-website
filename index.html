About Us

Virginia's Trusted Masonry Experts

G&W Contracting Services LLC is a family-owned Class A licensed contracting company based in Richmond, Virginia. Founded on the principles of craftsmanship, honesty, and hard work, we've built a reputation throughout the region for quality that stands the test of time.

Led by brothers George and Donnie Mundin, our team specializes in masonry — from residential patios and driveways to full commercial builds. We take on every project with the same commitment to excellence.
